Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna is a hearty dish with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned, then add marinara sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubble and golden.
