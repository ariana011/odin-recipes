Chicken Alfredo

Description

This creamy chicken alfredo is a comforting dish made with tender chicken, rich alfredo sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions; set aside
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in a skillet until golden brown and cooked through, then slice
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter and saute garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in parmesan cheese until melted and sauce thickens.
  6. Toss pasta in the sauce, then top with sliced chicken.
  7. Serve hot with additional parmesan if desired.
